Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe!

Let's bake some delicious chocolate chip cookies with the easiest recipe

Ingredients

  • 5 cups of flour
  • 5 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 6 eggs
  • 500 mg butter
  • 1 cup of milk
  • chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 300 degrees
  2. Mix all the dry ingredients in one bowl
  3. Mix all the wet ingredients in one bowl
  4. Combine both mixtures into one bowl and add a handful chocolate chips
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes
